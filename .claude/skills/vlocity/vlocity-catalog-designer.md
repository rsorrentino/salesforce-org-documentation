# Product Hierarchy

Recommended structure:

Commercial Offer  
↓  
Bundle  
↓  
Component Products  
↓  
Technical Services  

---

# Attribute Strategy

Attributes must be:

• reusable  
• categorized  
• configurable  

Avoid creating product-specific attributes unless required.

---

# Catalog Design Principles

Prefer:

Modular bundles  
Reusable attributes  
Shared pricing elements  

Avoid:

deep product nesting  
duplicated attributes  

---

# Catalog Scalability

Large telecom catalogs may contain:

10,000+ products  
complex bundles  
multiple pricing models  

Design catalogs with **reuse and modularity**.

catalog hierarchy
