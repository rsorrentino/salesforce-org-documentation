Data Model

Overview

The data model consists of standard Salesforce objects and custom objects designed for the medical area business processes.

Key Object Categories

  • Account Management: Account, Contact, Lead, Opportunity
  • Service Management: Service Appointment, Service Territory, Resource
  • Order Management: Order, OrderItem, Customer Order
  • Product Management: Product2, PricebookEntry
  • Case Management: Case, Task, Event
  • Medical Objects: ENT Doctor, Medical Shop, Fitting, Assessment
